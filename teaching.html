Who I Am

With nearly 10 years of experience in web design and development, I've acquired valuable knowledge in the tech field that I would like to pass on to others. I believe anyone can learn these skills and contribute to this amazing tool we call the Internet.

Beyond working with successful, award-winning tech companies and ad agencies over the years, I've also had the pleasure of building and teaching a web design curriculum for a city-wide coding school.

I pride myself in staying current with emerging trends and technologies. What I aim to teach you can become a foundation for you to build upon and literally create your own career.
